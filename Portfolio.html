'Teens of Denial' is my most recent short film, being mostly a deep dive into my psyche, being a very personal project of mine after original production plans went off the rails.

'Momentary Bliss' was a short photo series and short film done during the inital covid-19 pandemic. The work is a representation of myself trying to find happiness in a pretty dull point in time.

'Perceptible Cognitions' is a longer photo series that once again looks into my psyche in a point of time when things weren't so hot. While it isn't explicit in what I want to show and tell, it has subtle undertones and messages that people may pick up on, or not.

'Barracks' is my 3D enviroment I had created during my time in high school. This was the original idea for Goose McCain. Someone who fought in a war, and then I had the idea of making it a bloodied, zombie esc enviroment.

A 1911 pistol was one of the first proper 3D assets I had made for submission and completion of an assignment in high school.
